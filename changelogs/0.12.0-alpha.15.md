# Lunil 0.12.0-alpha.15

## Added

- Added a shareable `LuaPatchPreparationLimiter` with configurable concurrent isolated preflights
  and bounded queue capacity across rollout targets.
- Added zero, finite, and infinite admission waits for synchronous and asynchronous preparation,
  structured `Saturated` and `TimedOut` evidence, and live active/queued counters.

## Reliability

- Deferred preparation now stops before compilation, live-state revision binding, candidate
  execution, or replay reservation. Queue cancellation removes the waiter without consuming
  capacity, and every admitted path releases its lease after success, rejection, cancellation, or
  failure.
