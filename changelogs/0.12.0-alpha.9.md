# Lunil 0.12.0-alpha.9

## Added

- Added `ILuaPatchReplayStore`, an atomic check-and-record contract for deployment-wide replay
  protection.
- `LuaPatchPrepareOptions` can now apply `LuaPatchAcceptancePolicy` together with a replay store and
  time provider. Successful preparation records acceptance only after isolated preflight and live
  module binding succeed.

## Changed

- Concurrent preparation of the same patch identity now yields at most one prepared patch when the
  hosts share a conforming replay store. Policy mismatches and duplicates return structured
  `AcceptanceRejected` results before candidate execution.
