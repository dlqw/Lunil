# Lunil 0.9.0-alpha.3

## Tables and mixed loops

- Added guarded dense-integer and interned-field table operations to compiled numeric regions.
- Reused proven table and field bindings after their first guarded access.
- Preserved conservative invalidation at aliases, calls, metatables, and mutation boundaries.
- Allowed cold side calls around hot numeric loops without specializing unresolved calls.

## Performance

- Improved the `table_access` and `sieve` workloads while retaining deterministic fallback and
  canonical restart behavior.
