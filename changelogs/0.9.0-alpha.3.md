# Lunil 0.9.0-alpha.3

## Tables and mixed loops

- Keep guarded dense-integer and interned-field table operations inside compiled numeric regions.
- Forward exact same-block table reads from preceding writes while invalidating conservatively at
  aliases and call boundaries.
- Reuse execution-local table and field bindings after their first guarded access.
- Publish table content versions without a locked read-modify-write under the table's single-writer
  mutation contract, while preserving cache-reader visibility and metamethod invalidation.
- Admit cold side calls around hot numeric loops without admitting unresolved calls that are
  themselves hot.

## Performance qualification

The six-RID qualification uses six balanced rounds and a 250 ms calibration target. Auto JIT
reaches `0.457x` native Lua on `table_access` and `0.501x` on `sieve`, compared with `0.299x` and
`0.059x` in `0.8.0`. Auto and explicit Tier 2 retain stable compiled routes, correct results, and
clean fallback/deoptimization telemetry on every workload and release RID.

Backend qualification also passes conformance, differential execution, NativeAOT, trimming,
package/API, startup, allocation-growth, compilation-time, and generated-code regression gates.
