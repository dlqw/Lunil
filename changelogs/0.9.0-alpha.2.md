# Lunil 0.9.0-alpha.2

## JIT compilation economy

- Reuse canonical IR verification and natural-loop analysis for the lifetime of an immutable
  module without retaining modules after their normal lifetime.
- Replace per-region label dictionaries with compact ordered label maps, and emit semantic or
  direct-call fallback blocks only for instructions that can reach them.
- Remove transient iterator and immutable-builder allocations from numeric reaching-definition,
  successor, budget, and kind-state planning.

## Performance qualification

- Enforce Tier 2 and Loop OSR compilation-time, allocation, and per-instruction allocation-growth
  limits in backend qualification decisions.
- Compare unchanged Tier 2 and Loop OSR execution allocation and generated-code estimates with the
  published `0.8.0` six-RID backend baseline.
- Require unchanged Loop OSR routes to retain at least `0.95x` startup performance, at most `1.05x`
  execution allocation, and at most `1.15x` estimated generated code.
